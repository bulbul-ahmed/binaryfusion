Why companies hire us — and keep us.

The people you meet write the code.

We're a deliberately small senior team. There is no sales layer, no handoff to juniors after the contract is signed. The engineers on your first call are the engineers in your repository.

We run products of our own.

Binary Fusion builds and operates its own SaaS products alongside client work. It changes how we build for you: we think about churn, support load, and upgrade paths — because we live with them too.

You own everything from day one.

Your repository, your cloud account, your IP. From the first commit, everything we build is yours to take in-house, audit, or hand to another team. No hostages.

We stay after launch.

Our first rental client went live in 2023 and still ships new features with us today. Most engagements turn into long-term partnerships, because the platforms keep growing.
